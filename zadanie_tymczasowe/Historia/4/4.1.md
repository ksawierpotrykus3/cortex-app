### PARAMETRYZACJA ARCHITEKTONICZNA (4.1)

- **WATCHER_STABILITY_THRESHOLD** = `2000ms` (awaitWriteFinish stabilityThreshold)
- **WATCHER_POLL_INTERVAL** = `100ms` (awaitWriteFinish pollInterval)
- **WATCHER_IGNORED_PATTERNS** = `[\\/]Buffers[\\/]`, `[\\/]\.tmp$`, `[\\/]\.compact\.tmp$`, `\.lock$`, `ignoreDotFiles: true`
- **WATCHER_READY_TRIGGER** = ReadyEvent `{ filePath, size, timestamp }` emitowany po 2000ms stabilności bajtowej
- **WATCHER_LIFECYCLE** = `start()` → `chokidar.watch` → `destroy()` → `watcher.close()` + `removeAllListeners()`
